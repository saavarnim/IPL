Welcome to the Refining CAPTCHA Project!



Welcome to the Refining CAPTCHA Project, where we explore innovative and accessible ways to improve the user experience in verifying human interactions online. CAPTCHA (Completely Automated Public Turing test to tell Computers and Humans Apart) has become an essential security tool, but traditional methods can be challenging for some users. Our project focuses on enhancing CAPTCHA mechanisms, making them more inclusive, engaging, and effective.

About our Project

Why Refine CAPTCHA?

  • Accessibility: Traditional CAPTCHA methods like distorted text or image recognition can be difficult for people with disabilities or those with varying cognitive abilities. We aim to create CAPTCHA systems that are more inclusive.
  • User-Friendly Design: By integrating game-based elements such as drag-and-drop puzzles, we make CAPTCHA more enjoyable and less intrusive.
  • Security: While improving the user experience, we also ensure the CAPTCHA remains secure against automated bots, providing robust protection for online forms and accounts.

Features of Our CAPTCHA System

  • Game-Based Challenges: Users will interact with simple puzzles like shape-matching, color-matching, and drag-and-drop, making the verification process more engaging.
  • Bot Detection: We use advanced detection mechanisms like cursor movement analysis and time-based heuristics to differentiate between humans and bots.
  • Multiple Puzzle Types: Users can solve different types of puzzles, and we store these options to ensure variety and prevent pattern recognition by automated systems.

How Does It Work?

1. When you try to access a page, you will be prompted with a CAPTCHA challenge.

2. Choose from different puzzle types and solve them by interacting with drag-and-drop elements or matching shapes and colors.

3. After successfully solving the CAPTCHA, you will be redirected to your intended page.
